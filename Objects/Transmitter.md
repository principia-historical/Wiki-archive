# Transmitter
Transmit a signal over a frequency.

If **`IN1`** is used, the used frequency is shifted by +**`IN1`***10 rounded to the nearest whole value.

- **`IN0`**: The signal to transmit.
- **`IN1`**: Frequency shift.
